25+ Years of Guiding Families Home

Kirti Associates LLP is a leading real estate consulting firm based in Pune, committed to helping people find their perfect home and smart investments for over 25 years. With a strong foundation of trust, transparency, and client-first approach, we’ve guided 10,000+ families and investors to properties that match their needs, lifestyle, and dreams.

Founded by Mrs. Kirti Bhosale, a passionate and award-winning realtor, the company has grown into one of Pune’s most trusted names in real estate consulting. Her dedication and vision have earned national recognition, including awards like “Women Achiever in Property Consulting” and “Best Woman Realtor.”

From compact city apartments to spacious villas, from NA plots to commercial spaces — we provide end-to-end solutions with complete peace of mind.
